Yeosu

Yeosu is one my favorite cities in Korea.
This small city is the right one when you're thinking about taking a break from life.

The 3 exact attraction in Yeosu that I recommend is:

  • Odongdo: you can experience gorgeous sea view within a small forest.
  • Ganjang-Gejang: needless to say, EAT IT, even if you have an allergy for crabs.
  • Yeosu night sea: it's a classic.
Here is a masterplan for you: get to Yeosu, eat Ganjang-Gejang for lunch, go to Odongdo by bicycle, appreciate the nature there.
